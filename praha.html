Praha

Czech rep.

I was very excited for this trip. And I had a lot of fun in the capital of Czech republic!
出發前，我非常期待去捷克的首都!果然，結果沒有令我失望。

Hi everyone! This story is about my trip to Prague. It's a wonderful historic town.

大家好!布拉格是一個美麗的古城，我在那裡有很多很棒的經歷呢!

Let's start with most famous places - on my right you can see Prague Castle.

你知道布拉格最有名的景點是甚麼?那是布拉格城堡，就在我的右手面。

We went to Prague in winter. There was a huge Christmas market on Old Town Square.

每年的冬季，古城中心都會舉辦聖誕市集，規模可不少!

I couldn't wait for a show on famous Astronomic Clock on Old Town Square.
人們都聚集在天文鐘前，等待看表演，我也十分興奮呢!

Czech republic is famous for their beer. We visited an old Prague's Brewery called U Fleků. It's history started 500 years ago and they still brew beer based on their family recipe.

捷克啤酒十分馳名，所以我們去了其中一間歷史悠久的釀酒廠——U Fleků。這間釀酒廠有五百多年 的歷史，時至今天，他們仍使用家傳秘方釀製啤酒。

Talking about beer, I got hungry. Let's check out some Czech food!

說起啤酒，我就肚子餓了!大家一起看看捷克有甚麼好東西吃!

Roasted pork with potato dumplings
這是烤豬肉和蒸薯仔麵包
Pork knuckle on right, roasted pork neck with Czech dumplings on left.
右面是豬手和酸菜，左面是烤豬頸肉和蒸麵包
It's a goulash soup served in bread.
麵包匈牙利湯(Goulash Soup)

Let's see what you can find in the Christmas Market!

看看我在聖誕市集發現了甚麼!

This was everywhere! There's nothing better that east fresh and crispy chimney cake. In Czech, it's called Trdelník. Can you pronounce that?
烤麵包圈(Trdelník)，這種捷克傳統小吃隨處可見，新鮮出爐的非常好吃，外脆內嫩呢!它的捷克語 名字很難發音，你們都試試看吧!
We're in Central Europe, so there's also maaany sausages.
身處中歐，當然少不了香腸!

Now, let's make an introduction to the best Czech snacks - they were so tasty!

捷克的零嘴都很美味，你試過了嗎?

Czechs are good in making their own chips. They are much crispier than those you might know!
捷克的薯片原來挺有名的，比我吃過的都更香脆!
This was my most favorite strawberry yoghurt!
這是我最最最愛的士多啤梨乳酪!

Enough of food, let's explore the city again!

我們都吃飽飽，是時候再去探索一下城市!

Me on the Charles Bridge! There ware more people than on The Great Wall of China.
我站在查理大橋上!查理大橋上的遊人，比中國萬里長城的還要多。
The most beautiful view in Prague - capturing Charles Bridge and Castle.
查理大橋和城堡構成布拉格最美麗的風景，這亦是拍攝布拉格的最佳位置。
You can see the National Theatre behind me
在我身後的是國家大劇院。
Prague has many churches and cathedrals. But this architectural style was the weirdest! And look at the tower. You can find it in Prague's Vinohrady neighbourhood.
布拉格城內有很多教堂，但這間教堂的外形最奇怪!不遠處還有一座塔。如果你想看看這些建築 物，那便見走到 Vinohrady 區了。

There was much more to do in Prague - we had long walks in romantic streets of Old Town, visited Kafka Museum, tried a lot of great food and beers. It was much more than I can cover in this story.

布拉格真的很好玩!我們走過了浪漫的大街小巷、探訪卡夫卡博物館、嚐了很多傳統美食和啤酒，可惜我的遊記不能一一盡錄呢?

That's all for today, read my next story, my friends!

今天就到此為止啦。朋友仔，繼續看看我下一個故事。
