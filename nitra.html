Nitra

It was a cold January's day when me and my friends went to this small town in Slovakia.
當我和我的朋友去斯洛伐克的這個小鎮時，那是一月的寒冷。

Nitra is one of the oldest cities in slovakia. With a population about 80,000 it is the fifth-largest city in Slovakia.

尼特拉是斯洛伐克最古老的城市之一。人口約8萬，是斯洛伐克第五大城市。

This photo was taken when we were leaving the castle.
這張照片是我們離開城堡時拍攝的。

In town centre we dicovered a charming book store. As we entered, we found an illustrated book about Hong Kong.

在市中心，我們發現了一家迷人的書店。 當我們進入時，我們找到一本關於香港的插圖。

We had a tasty lunch in this welcoming place.

我們在這個歡迎的地方吃了一頓美味的午餐。

It was a wonderful day. In local bakery we dicovered a cake called Gaštanové jazýčky - chesnut tongues. Wasn't as tasty as my favorite strawberries, but I enjoyed that.

這是一個美好的一天。 在當地的麵包店，我們發現了一種名叫Gaštanové jazýčky的蛋糕 - 栗子舌頭。 沒有我最喜歡的草莓好吃，但我很喜歡。

That's all for today, read my next story, my friends!

這就是今天，閱讀我的下一個故事，我的朋友們！
