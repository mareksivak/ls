Nitra

It was a cold January's day when me and my friends went to this small town in Slovakia.
在一個寒冷的一月天，我跟我的朋友走訪了斯洛伐克的一個小鎮。

Nitra is one of the oldest cities in slovakia. With a population about 80,000 it is the fourth-largest city in Slovakia.

尼特拉人口約有八萬，是斯洛伐克第四大城市，亦是斯洛伐克歷史最悠久的城市之一。

This photo was taken when we were leaving the castle.
這張照片是我們離開城堡時拍攝的。

In town centre we discovered a charming book store. As we entered, we found an illustrated book about the old Hong Kong.

在小鎮的中心，我們發現了一家很有特色的書店。在那裏，我們找到了一本關於老香港的畫冊

We had a tasty lunch in this welcoming place.

我們在這裡吃了一頓美味的午餐。看﹗這裡的裝潢很有心思呢﹗

It was a wonderful day. In (a) local bakery we discovered (discovered) a cake called Gaštanové jazýčky - chesnut tongues. Wasn't as tasty as my favorite (favourite) strawberries, but I enjoyed that.

真是美好的一天﹗在一家麵包店，我們發現了一種叫 Gaštanové jazýčky 的糕點。雖然這 個由栗子造的糕點沒有士多啤梨般美味，但我都很喜歡呢﹗

That's all for today, read my next story, my friends!

今天就到此為止啦。朋友仔，繼續看看我下一個故事。
