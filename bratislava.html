Bratislava

Slovakia

Hi everyone! For those of my friends, who've never heard of this city, Bratislava is a capital of Slovakia. And Slovakia is a small country in the heart of Europe. I will tell you how much fun I had here!

大家知道布拉提斯拉瓦嗎?布拉提斯拉瓦(Bratislava)是斯洛伐克的首都，而斯洛伐克是中歐的一個小國。我在那裡玩得不亦樂乎!

Look at this guy! When I was passing him, he stopped me and asked to take a photo. So I said ok!

看看這小伙子!當我經過他身旁時，他問我可否跟我拍照，那當然沒問題啦!

This guy is staring from canal! There ware so many tourists taking photo with him. So I asked him why he's not at work.

很多遊客跟這水渠小子拍照呢!我覺得很納悶，為甚麼這小伙子躲在水渠裡盯着人呢?於是我走過 去問他:「你為甚麼不去工作?」

I'm getting tired of walking. I have an idea - let's get some hot chocolate!

走着走着，都走得累啦。啊!不如去喝些熱朱古力!

Then we stopped for a cup of hot chocolate. We stopped at Bistro Mondieu. I highly recommend to visit!

By the way, I had a good talk with a guy on the right. He told me he also likes strawberries. Hohoho!

我們找到了一間很不錯的餐廳——Bistro Mondieu，在那裡我點了一杯非常可口的熱朱古力，推薦給大家!

還有，我跟那個穿盔甲的士兵談了一會兒天，他告訴我他也很喜歡士多啤梨!呵呵呵可!

On our way to Danube River we met this kind gentleman - his name is Schöne Náci. He offered me his hat. It was too big for me.

然後，在出發到多瑙河的路上，我們遇到了這位紳士，他的名字是 Schöne Náci。他邀請我試戴他的 帽子，但是太大了。

Look at this - it's Bratislava Castle behind me! It stands on a top of the hill, from Old Town it takes you 15 mins walk throughout old streets.

看!這是我在布拉提斯拉瓦城堡拍的照片!城堡位於山頂上，從舊城區出發，穿過老街，大約十五 分鐘便可到達。

It's me on Castle Hill. It was sunny so I thought it's a good idea to get some sunshine on my belly. Hohoho!

我登上了城堡山。這天陽光明媚，所以我認為讓我的肚子曬曬太陽是一個好主意。呵呵呵呵!

Bratislava is not a big city, but its development is very dynamic. It builds its modern site while preserves its historical heritage. Its Old Town is small compared to cities like Prague or Vienna, but it has its own charm and a lot to offer!

雖然布拉提斯拉瓦不是一個大城市，但十分有活力。在發展的同時，亦妥善保護了城內的歷史文 物。與布拉格或維也納的舊城區相比，布拉提斯拉瓦的比較小，但它也很有魅力，等待我們去發掘 更多!
